    FACILITIES


    The Civil Engineering Laboratories are located in the Lab-1 block in our permanent campus, Yerpadu. The laboratory is of size 5400 sq. ft having facilities to instruct UG laboratory classes and conduct high quality research. The following are the major laboratory facilities present:

    • Building Materials Laboratory
    • Environmental Engineering Laboratory
    • Geotechnical Engineering Laboratory
    • Hydraulics & Water Resources Engineering Laboratory
    • Structural Engineering Laboratory
    • Surveying Laboratory
    • Transportation Engineering: Advanced Pavement Systems (APS) Laboratory
